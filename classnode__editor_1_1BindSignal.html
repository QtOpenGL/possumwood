Detailed Description

A boost-compatible signal binding object, based on http://uint32t.blogspot.co.uk/2008/11/using-boost-bind-and-boost-function.html . Allows to use boost::bind and std::function (0-argument resulting functor) to be bound as a 0-argument slot.
